Our House Rules are getting a refresh for 2022

We regularly take a look at our policies (or as they’re affectionately known around here, our House Rules) to ensure that they’re still serving our community’s needs as best they can. We’re happy to report that improvements are being made to our House Rules on March 9. To learn more about these changes before they take effect next month, keep reading.

Increased transparency

We’ve refreshed and reorganized several policies—like our Privacy Policy, Payments Policy, Electronic Comms Policy, and Buyer Policy—to increase transparency, provide more clarity, and made updates to reflect new features and services.

Clearer Requests for Information Policy

Etsy requires a valid and sufficient legal process to disclose information about an Etsy member. We’ve updated our Requests for Information Policy with the aim of providing more transparency to our members. Specifically, we’ve updated this policy to outline how, in situations where information is validly requested, Etsy will contact you before disclosing your information (unless it’s a legal situation where we’re prohibited to do so).

Read the full updated policies

Before we go: We know that some of you may have opted out of emails from us, but we’re required to let you know about these policy changes no matter what. To learn more about notifications from Etsy, check out this Help Center article.
